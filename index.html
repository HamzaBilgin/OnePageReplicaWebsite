OUR MANUFACTURING PROCESS

SUSTAINABILITY

  • Fabric & materials

    Fabric choice is an important element in our design process and we only use the highest quality European, Japanese or Korean fabrics.

  • Designed for life

    We don’t believe in fast fashion or throw away culture. To ensure our pieces will be worn for a lifetime we create clothes which have good drape, are non-sheer and feel comfortable to the skin.

  • Manufacture

    When it comes to pollution, the manufacturing process is the biggest contributing step in our cycle. We continue to invest in new technological solutions which aim to reduce our carbon footprint and revolutionise the way we make clothes.

  • SOCIAL

    From the farmer producing our cotton, to the retail clerk ringing you up, we are committed to fair treatment for all of our people. To ensure this we promote transparency so you can find out information about your garment at every step of the cycle.
